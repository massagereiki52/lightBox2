Illustrator & Photoshop Fun

Since starting school I have created various projects using Illustrator and Photoshop. My favorite program out of the two is Illustrator. I love the versatility and flexability within this program.I love to use it to create images for my web backgrounds, other content for my sites as well as other printed artwork. The best thing about both photoshop and illustrator is that you can flip flop between both programs when creating content. There are multiple functions of each that correspond with the other. I love to use it to create images for my web backgrounds, other content for my sites as well as other printed artwork.

Photoshop isn't so bad either. When I first started school and learning about Photoshop it was a bit of a struggle, but now that I'm getting towards the end of my program I have started to really like this program as well. I love to bring my photos into a program called Lightroom before going into Photoshop, because I like to make my minor adjustments and then I will make my more intricate and finite adjustments in Photoshop.
